Discover Ohrid

Nestled on the shores of one of Europe's oldest and deepest lakes, Ohrid is a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its historical architecture, clear waters, and stunning mountain backdrop. Our catamaran tours offer a unique perspective of this ancient city and its breathtaking surroundings.
